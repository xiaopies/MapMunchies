About

 

Info:

              The goal of MapMunchies is to help small food businesses that have been struggling due to the pandemic! People with stories to share about a personal experience or memory relating to their favorite small businesses can post them on our interactive map. Users can contact each other to meet up (most likely virtually as of now) and enjoy food together! This way people can spread their love for food and support local businesses at the same time.
The results suggest that the pandemic had already caused massive dislocation among small businesses just several weeks after its onset and prior to the availability of government aid through the Coronavirus Aid, Relief, and Economic Security (CARES) Act. Across the full sample, 43% of businesses had temporarily closed, and nearly all of these closures were due to COVID-19.
